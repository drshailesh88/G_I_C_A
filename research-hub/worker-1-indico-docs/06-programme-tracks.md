# Indico — Defining the Programme (Tracks)

**Source:** https://indico.docs.cern.ch/conferences/programme/

**Image base URL:** https://indico.docs.cern.ch/assets/

---

## Overview

The Programme page defines the **tracks** of a conference. Tracks represent the subject matter and are used to classify submissions during the Call for Abstracts.

---

## Flow

1. Enter event **management** interface (pencil icon at top of display view)
2. Click **Programme** on left sidebar under **Organisation**
3. Click **Add track**
4. Enter track **title** and brief **description**
5. Click **Save**
6. Repeat for all tracks
7. Drag and drop tracks to reorder (grey rectangle handle at top of each track)

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Track** | A subject category for the conference (e.g., "Online computing", "Offline computing") |
| **Track ordering** | Drag and drop via grey handle at top of each track |
| **Prerequisite** | Tracks **must** be defined before opening the Call for Abstracts |
| **Purpose** | Submitters choose which track their abstract belongs to |

---

## Important Notes

- Tracks are NOT the same as sessions — tracks are for abstract classification, sessions are for timetable organization
- Track assignment happens during abstract submission
- After abstract acceptance, contributions may be assigned to sessions for scheduling
- The Programme page also has a **Configure** button for additional programme settings
