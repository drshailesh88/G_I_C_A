# Session 7: TravelPerk — Travel Itinerary Management
**Module:** Travel Info (#7)
**URL:** https://travelperk.com (free plan)
**Time:** 1 hour

## Booking/Itinerary View
- Single-person travel itinerary layout
- Fields: departure, arrival, airline, PNR, ticket number, etc.
- Document attachment to booking (ticket PDF, e-ticket image)
- Date and time display format
- Timeline or calendar view

## Traveler Profile
- Information stored per traveler
- Trip association with event/project

## Admin/Organizer View
- All travelers for a specific trip/event
- Filters: travel date, destination, status
- Export capabilities
- Summary views (e.g., "5 people arriving March 10")

## Notifications
- Traveler confirmations
- Itinerary email appearance
- Change alerts

**Our use case:** Event coordinator creates travel records per delegate (from/to, departure/arrival, PNR, ticket attachment) then sends personalized itinerary emails. Focus on data fields and per-person itinerary display.
